About Us

The MD Educational Institute is an institution dedicated to providing comprehensive education through regular, distance, online mode.

The institute offers a wide range of academic programs, including 10th or Secondary, +2 or Senior Secondary, UG undergraduate and PG postgraduate degrees, Teacher Education programs D. El. Ed, B. Ed, B.P.Ed, M. Ed, NTT, certificate courses, diplomas, and postgraduate diplomas. These programs cover diverse fields such as Schooling Education, Engineering & Technology, Commerce and Management Studies, Computer Science & IT, Hotel Management, Library & Information Science, Science, Arts & Social Studies, Foreign & Indian Languages, Teacher Education, Journalism & Mass Communication, Law, Pharmacy, Para-Medical Science, and Fire & Safety.

MD Educational Institute is a Govt of India Registered Organization UDYAM-OD-15-0021475. ISO Certified Number, Affiliated to The Board of Open Schooling and Skill Education, Sikkim ( BOSSE), Authorised Study Centre of OKCL, Govt of Odisha. And Partner with many Universities of India ( UGC Granted University, Government of India)
